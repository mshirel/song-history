About

What is this app?

Highland Church of Christ uses this app to track which songs are sung each Sunday. It reads the PowerPoint worship slides and automatically extracts song titles, credits, and service details.

This data is used to generate CCLI compliance reports (required for churches that project copyrighted song lyrics). The app also provides statistics on song frequency, leader preferences, and service history.
